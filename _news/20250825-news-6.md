Our very own Geovanny was busy this summer at Stanford and UC-Berkley being the lead math instructor for the DaVinci Camp Summer Institute, which is a summer program that seamlessly integrates social and academic components from the arts and applied sciences. Designed to develop the interests and abilities of talented and highly motivated middle school and high school students (rising 6th – 12th grades, including graduating seniors), while in session, students learn the skills necessary to understand complex math expressions and to reduce them to their fundamental parts. They learn techniques that transform  them into faster, more confident problem solvers. Congratulations, Geo!
